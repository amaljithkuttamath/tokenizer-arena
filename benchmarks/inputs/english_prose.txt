Transformer models have fundamentally changed the landscape of natural language processing. Since the publication of "Attention Is All You Need" in 2017, the architecture has become the foundation for nearly every state-of-the-art language model. The key innovation, self-attention, allows the model to weigh the importance of different parts of the input sequence when producing each output token. This mechanism replaced recurrent and convolutional approaches, enabling much greater parallelism during training. Modern large language models like GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini all build on this foundation, scaling to hundreds of billions of parameters trained on vast corpora of text from the internet.
